About Phi Donkam

Phi Donkam is a dedicated platform for the talented people of Meghalaya to showcase their skills, connect with opportunities, and build a thriving local economy. Our name "Phi Donkam" reflects the spirit of community and collaboration that defines our beautiful state.

Our Mission

To empower local artisans, freelancers, and service providers by providing them with a platform to reach customers, showcase their work, and grow their businesses while preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Meghalaya.

What We Offer

  • Easy-to-use platform for showcasing services and products
  • Location-based service discovery
  • Secure contact system for connecting service providers with customers
  • Support for traditional crafts and modern services alike
  • Mobile-friendly design for accessibility across all devices

Join Our Community

Whether you're a traditional weaver, photographer, musician, farmer, or tech professional, Phi Donkam is your platform to connect with customers and grow your business in Meghalaya.
